Heart Health São Paulo

For over 50 years, Heart Health São Paulo has been dedicated to saving lives by making a difference to the heart health of our people.

Donations help us fund valuable research, develop guidelines for health professionals, support patient care and help Paulistas to live healthier lifestyles.
